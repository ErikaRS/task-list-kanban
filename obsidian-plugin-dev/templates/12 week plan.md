How to use this: 
- At the beginning of a 12 week period, goals
	- Working backwards from each goal, break down tactics. Each of these should be a concrete action that has a yes/no outcome
	- Set due dates for each tactic
- Each week, 
	- On Monday, Move tactics for that week into tasks for the week
	- On Friday, score my execution (tactics completed vs planned)
	- On Friday, evaluate plan

# Goals
- High level goal
- Second goal
- AT MOST 3 goals
